Create

Transform sketches into refined shapes. The Create module lets you draw, arrange and refine shapes and lines with precision.

Shapes & Lines

Draw precise shapes and lines using a suite of pen, line and arc tools. Use snapping, alignment and layering to build up more complex compositions from simple elements. r3Design provides visual guides to keep proportions and spacing consistent as you work.

Adjust stroke widths, mirror elements and duplicate patterns to iterate quickly. Grouping and arrangement tools help you organize components for simulation and review.

Assemble & Align

Building complex prototypes requires assembling multiple parts. Use snapping and alignment tools to position components relative to each other. Group parts to move them collectively or nest assemblies inside larger structures. You can also copy and array parts along paths or patterns to quickly create repetitive structures.

When working with imported assets, use the alignment helpers to match coordinate systems and orient your parts correctly. A built-in measuring tool lets you verify clearances and fits, ensuring that everything aligns perfectly before moving to simulation.

Import & Modify

r3Design supports common design imports and allows you to bring sketches into the workspace for refinement. Once in the app, you can arrange, combine and review elements before simulation and export.

Our version control system tracks your design iterations, allowing you to revert or branch off at any point. This flexibility encourages experimentation without fear of losing progress.
